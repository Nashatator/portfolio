KOB

The goal of this project is to build a more efficient relationship between the number 1 cleaning company and its client and to sensitize the office employees to be more involved in the cleaning of their workplace. We decided (after a lot of interviews, analogies and field studies) to make a project that will mix what’s popular for people between 30 and 50 and which is really fun and easy to use.

Coming soon

Chiba-pack

The goal of this project was to make a packaging for Japanese people representing Chiba and it was really hard as a foreigner to do that since we don’t have the same aesthetic. I looked at my favorite things about Japan and I chose one of the shapes that appears often. I shaped it in different models, each one representing a Chiba’s speciality. I really wanted the packaging to appears pure and simple, for everybody not just the rich.

SAP

The goal of this project was to help an insurance company to gain more private practitioners as clients. During this project we spent a lot of time on the research by trying new methods like «what how why». By joining the tendancies with those research we decided to work on complementary money and chose the itinerant private practitioner as a target. This is how this project became an app that will transform your expenses accounts into bonuses to help your everyday life as a itinerant private practitioner.

Knowple

Coming soon

Knowple

This project was my first interaction project that I did during my second year at Strate. This project was about trying to imagine the future of libraries. There is bad in libraries and there is bad in having everything on your computer, so for this project I tried to mix the good of both in the form of a connected object. Knowple will allow you to have all your contents safe and together but in a way that you can still watch it whenever and wherever you want.
